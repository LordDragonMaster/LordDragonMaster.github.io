Differences Between Characters

Ok so these characters are not actually identical. They have a few key differences:

  • Dave(Cyan Square) cannot jump off of Bounce Pads. This is to balance the ability the squares have to jump off of one another. Also i forgot to add it.
  • Dave can't be launched by the Level 2 cannon.
  • Dave explodes in Purple instead of cyan because reasons.
  • Generally, turrets will prefer to target Bob over Dave.
Other than that, heres some general common sense!
  • In Level 2, get the ball into the cannon to win! (note: if you don't win, then I probobly messed something up. Whoops. I blame George Bush and standardised schooling.)
  • Turrets shoot cannonballs, but they shoot them badly. If you stand still, they usually can't hit you.
  • In Level 1, you need to move the bounce pad in order to collect the powerup.
  • Get a running start on the ball in order to punt it a decent distance.
